The history of esports

The earliest forms of competitive gaming can be traced back to the 1970s, with events like the "Intergalactic Spacewar Olympics" in 1972 at Stanford University. The prize was a year's subscription to Rolling Stone magazine. The 21st century marked a significant consolidation of esports, with the professionalization of teams, coaches, and training regimens, mirroring traditional sports. Platforms like Twitch and YouTube became crucial for broadcasting and fan engagement.
